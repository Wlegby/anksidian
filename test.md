# Thermal expansion
## Definition
- Change of ==[[volume]]/length== in response to ==change of
[[temperature]]==
## Formula
==$$Delta V/L = V/L dot gamma/alpha dot Delta T$$==
## Coefficients
==$gamma$== = ==linear==, ==$alpha$== = ==volumetric coefficient==
### Unit
==$\left\lbrack \frac \gamma \alpha \right\rbrack = 1K^{-1}\$==
